  5. What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?

    I think that the primary goal of the interactive experience is to exhibit his work and the skills that he has, in order to perhaps get a job. As employers may visit his website and can see what he has to offer to them and they can easily contact him through his 'profiles' page.

  6. How does the interactive experience communicate this primary goal?

    The interactive experience communicates its primary goal by stimulating the visitors to explore his skills. He does this by using movement on the 'explore me' button. Also, by stating 'portfolio' and 'profiles 'clearly and multiple times, it is obvious that he wants to show his work and be contacted.

The multiple mentions of 'portfolio' (in the menu above and the menu which appears when you click the 'explore me' button) is also a good way to direct people to the most important content of your website, as this is the main thing he wants to show.
